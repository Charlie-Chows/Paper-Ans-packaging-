Frequently Asked Specifications

What is the standard MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?

For custom folding carton boxes, our MOQ is 1,000 units. For high-end luxury rigid boxes, our MOQ is 500 units.

How do I approve structural box proofs?

Our engineers upload structural CAD vector proofs to the Design Vault. You can review them, request print alterations, and check status marks inside the portal.

Can I request physical sample boxes?

Yes! Unprinted structural sample proofs can be ordered during the CAD verification phase to test bottle/product fitting.
