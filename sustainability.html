Material Specifications

Raw Paperboards & Structural Fibers

We partner with top raw board mills to harvest premium unbleached papers, dense greyboards, and white bleached sulfate stocks.

Standard Kraft

Heavy unbleached kraft board sheets, perfect for corrugated shipping mailers and structural folding flaps.

Rigid Greyboard

Heavyweight 1200gsm laminated greyboard sheets, forming the structural core of luxury gift boxes.

Bleached SBS

Solid Bleached Sulfate sheets with white clay coating, optimal for high-fidelity retail sleeve runs.

Die-Cut Dielines

We construct structural products with foldable joints and tuck flaps, avoiding chemical adhesive glues.

Structural Calibration

Lifecycle of our Packaging runs

We engineer our custom boxes from die template design to final press assembly checking.

01

CAD Dieline Design

Our engineers formulate vector dielines based on exact product dimensions. We calculate folding tolerances and joint clearance parameters to guarantee snug fits.

02

Rigid Board Cutting

We operate precision rotary die cutters to slice heavy greyboard sheets or corrugated boards with clean, dust-free edges.

03

Custom Foil & Stamping

We heat metallic foils (gold, silver, copper) under immense pressure to transfer premium decorative details directly onto board surfaces.

04

Fulfillment & Assembly

Once printed, our boxes are packed flat and delivered directly to your warehousing locations, ready for quick manual assembly.
