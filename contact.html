Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard minimum order volume starts at 500 units per size/configuration for printed retail sleeves and mailing boxes, and 1,000 units for rigid luxury gift boxes due to setup tooling.

Do you offer physical samples?

Yes, we fabricate both structural CAD unprinted samples (to verify fit and fold mechanism) and high-fidelity printed mockups with complete foil coatings. Please request samples using our contact form.

What is your production lead time?

Once digital mockups and structures are approved, production takes approximately 10 to 14 business days. Trans-ocean cargo shipping takes an additional 15 to 20 business days depending on location.
