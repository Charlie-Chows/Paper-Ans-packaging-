Our Heritage

A Century of Innovation & Design

Artic Paper was born from a simple idea: that beautiful packaging delivers striking results. Over the decades, we have evolved from a small traditional mill into a global leader in high-end structural packaging design and manufacturing.

Every fiber we select, every dye we formulate, and every box we craft represents our dedication to absolute premium quality. We partner with the world's most visionary brands to turn packaging into an unboxing ritual.

Our Values

The Principles That Shape Us

Core beliefs that guide our design choices and production processes every single day.

Craftsmanship

Uncompromising focus on texture, finish, and structural integrity. Every detail is calibrated for luxury and brand expression.

Material Grade

Top-tier unbleached Kraft, laminated greyboards, and SBS boards. We select weights and grades for absolute durability and product protection.

Innovation

Pioneering custom dielines, folding mechanisms, and spot UV finishes to maximize packaging display impact.
